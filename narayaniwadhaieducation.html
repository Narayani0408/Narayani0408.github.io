Milestones of Education

April 2022

Tejswini Vidya Mandir 

My school is a place of learning, growth, and discipline. It has a beautiful campus with spacious classrooms, a library, computer labs, and a playground. The teachers are very supportive and always encourage us to do our best in studies as well as extracurricular activities. Various events, cultural programs, and sports are organized to develop our skills and confidence. My school is like a second home where I not only gain knowledge but also learn values like respect, honesty, and teamwork.

June 2024

ST. Paul Junior College

My junior college was an important phase in my academic journey. It acted as a bridge between school and professional studies, preparing me for higher education. The teachers were highly knowledgeable and guided us with both academics and career choices. Along with classroom learning, I gained confidence through seminars, projects, and group activities. It was a place where I built strong friendships, discovered my interests, and learned the value of hard work and discipline. My junior college truly shaped my foundation for pursuing B.Tech.

June 2028

S. B. Jain Institute of Technology

I am currently pursuing my engineering degree at S. B. Jain Institute of Technology, Management and Research (SBJITMR), Nagpur. The college provides a dynamic learning environment with modern infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, and experienced faculty members who always guide and motivate us. Apart from academics, the institute encourages innovation, research, and participation in technical as well as cultural events. The campus has excellent facilities like a library, computer labs, and sports grounds, creating a balanced atmosphere for learning and growth. Studying at SB Jain is helping me strengthen my technical knowledge, develop problem-solving skills, and prepare for a successful career in engineering.
